The fixed term contract is the contract that has a start date and an end date. The contractual relationship starts with the commencement date and ends with the expiry date of the contract. <br>
While the unlimited contract starts with the joining date of the employee and ends with the statuary notification of the employee or the employer to terminate the contract. Saudi Employees contract are unlimited contract after three years of their joining date. Non-Saudi employees are not eligible for unlimited contract.
